Salt City Optics Style Guide

Style guides are designed to create a consistent and fluid brand experience between devices, browsers, and other digital assets. Maintaining an up-to-date guide for typography, icons, forms, and other web components is critical for an modern website.

Typography

There are two primary fonts being used on the site; Brandon Grotesque and Proxima Nova. Brandon Grotesque is use for the headings and offers a touch of character without being too bold or gaudy. Proxima Nova is a stalwart font that was designed for the web. It provided excellent readability without being boring or overused.

Iconography

Salt City Optic uses the icon set “Entypo” as its primary source for icons. This set has a modern aesthetic with a round and light feel that pairs well with Brandon Grotesque and Proxima Nova.

Color

Salt City Optic uses a simple color palette that raises through a mostly white background. This simple use of color keeps the design clean and allows important elements to speak for themselves without being obnoxious.
