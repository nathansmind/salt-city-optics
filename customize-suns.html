Single Vision

Features one single correction to remedy nearsightedness or farsightedness.

Progressive

Features 3 different corrections in one gradual progression from top to bottom: far vision, intermediate vision, and near vision. Also known as a lineless bifocal.

Standard

These 100% UVA-B Blocking lenses come in clear, or non-polarized tints.

Polarized

A polarization filter in the lens serves to block harmful glare from water, snow, ice, and the road.

Transitions

These lenses adapt to light, going from clear when indoors, to medium-darkness sunglasses when outdoors in direct sunlight.

Transitions Vantage

These lenses adapt to light going from clear indoors, to medium-darkness, polarized sunglasses when outdoors.

Transitions Xtractive

These lenses adapt to light, going from clear indoors to dark sunglasses in direct sunlight. Xtractive Transitions will also transition in the car.

Brown

Ideal for mid-bright conditions when enhanced contrast is necessary.

Grey

Perfect for bright conditions when neutral color perception is desired.

Grey Green

Neutral color perception with added contrast, ideal for bright conditions.

Rose

Recommended for mid-bright conditions where contrast is critical.

Silver

Silver is a neutral mirror that looks best on grey and rose base tints. It looks like a black mirror, but a bit more reflective.

Blue

Blue mirrors look great on grey and brown base tints.

Red

Red mirrors are ideal on any base tints as they are very bold and opaque.

Green

Green mirrors often have gold undertones, and look best on brown or grey-green lenses.

Bronze

Bronze mirrors are neutral in appearance and look best on brown lenses.

Black

Black mirrors are neutral and look good on all base tints.

Anti-Reflective

A/R coatings let more light through the lens to reduce glare and allow lookers on to see your eyes, rather than light reflecting off your lenses.

Premium Anti-reflective

Premium Anti Reflective Coating offers the features of the Anti-Reflective coating, plus an improved anti-scratch coating that is harder to smudge and stays clean longer.

Blue Shield

A harmful blue-light blocking lens that combats computer eye strain, eye fatigue, and macular degeneration. The best Anti-Reflective coating we offer!
